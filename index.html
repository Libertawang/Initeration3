Independence Day

Indonesia got its Independence on the 17th of August, 1945 after being colonized for over 350 years

To celebrate this day, each year Indonesians would gather around their local neighbourhoods and play "Panjat Pinang".

About Us!

Indonesian Intelligence is a website that helps people get to know the culture of Indonesia.

We consider each person to be unique and cater to their specific needs by providing personalized solutions.

The website aims to inspire and support first generation born Indonesian Australians through their journey of knowing their parent's culture.
